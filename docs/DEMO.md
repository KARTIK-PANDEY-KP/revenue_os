# Demo script (~90 seconds)

Runs fully in mock mode — no keys needed. With keys, the same flow uses live data.

1. **Land on the Dashboard.** Hot accounts, fresh signals, today's priorities, and a
   live signal ticker across the masthead. Cursor sits at the top, score **94**.
2. **Research a company.** Type `Cursor` in the research bar → **Research**.
   Bright Data gathers web data, signals are extracted, the account is scored, and
   Cognee stores it. You're routed to the account intelligence page.
3. **Read the dossier.** Header shows fit / intent / timing / risk sub-scores and a
   big **"Why now"** callout. Tabs: Overview, Signals, People, Outreach, Calls,
   Timeline, Risk. Click **Ask memory** to see Cognee's "why is this a priority?"
4. **Generate a sequence.** Hit **Sequence** → a 4–6 step, multi-day cadence is
   generated, first email pre-written. **Launch** dispatches the Trigger.dev workflow.
5. **Prospect by ICP.** Go to **Prospects**, type
   *"Series A AI infrastructure startups hiring sales engineers"* → discovered,
   researched, ranked accounts with decision-makers and openers.
6. **Make a call.** From the account hit **Call** (or open **Dialer**). The room opens
   (LiveKit), and the call begins. Speechmatics transcribes live; when the prospect
   says *"too expensive"* or *"we use Apollo"*, the **AI copilot** fires an
   instant battlecard.
7. **End the call.** Get an AI **summary**, a **scorecard** (discovery, objection
   handling, personalization, next-step, talk ratio), and a follow-up draft — all
   written to memory. The call-completed workflow ran end-to-end.
8. **Coach.** **Coaching** shows the scorecard breakdown, transcript, rep leaderboard,
   and an **AI roleplay** trainer to practice against a skeptical VP.
9. **Settings.** See every sponsor integration's live/mock status at a glance.

### One-liner

> *"RevenueOS found Cursor, told me why they matter this week, ranked them #1, wrote
> the outreach, ran the call with a live copilot, scored my performance, and queued
> the follow-up — Bright Data found it, Cognee remembered it, Trigger.dev acted on it,
> LiveKit + Speechmatics ran the conversation, and Claude reasoned over all of it."*
